Critical Decision Points

Honest Assessment Required

Before significant investment, validate that users actually want AI companions vs optimization tools. The emotional connection concept needs real-world testing with actual gamers, not just theoretical appeal.

Market Reality Check

Gaming AI tools typically serve optimization (better performance) or convenience (automation). Companionship is a newer value proposition that needs careful validation with target users.

Sustainable Scope

Focus on perfecting one game (D2) rather than building a platform initially. Success with a single, beloved game proves the concept and funds expansion.
