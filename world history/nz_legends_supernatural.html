Dargaville: Gateway to the Mystical North

Situated on the Northern Wairoa River in the Kaipara District, Dargaville serves as the gateway to some of New Zealand's most spiritually charged landscapes. This unassuming town of 4,500 souls sits at the convergence of ancient pathways and supernatural highways.

The largest of the three lakes, Taharoa is said to house a protective taniwha who ensures the lakes remain pure. Swimmers occasionally report feeling watched or touched by unseen hands in the deeper sections.
